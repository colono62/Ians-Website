Ideas for your Webpage!

In terms of ideas, there's a lot of possibilities for a website. One of the more broad applications i can see being used by the masses is a blog. It looks a lot more professional and, as a plus, you actually own your content. Another great application for the masses, a resume. Helpful Resources

Here are some resources for HTML and CSS, be it for learning how to code or for finding free images or music.

  • W3Schools. As was mentioned previously, it is a great tool for learning commands, how they work and perhaps a solution to your problem. It's also a great tutorial.
  • Old School Runescape and Runescape music. The music has won awards for the sheer amount of tracks there are. Old School Runescape has retro MIDI tracks and Runescape has beautiful award winning modern tracks. Both are copyright free so you can't go wrong for the purposes of making a website with these.
  • Flickr. On Flickr you can sort by copyright restrictions and find images to use on your projects. Every image on this website except my dog and the logo was retrieved from Flickr.
